Formatting Text

To make text go in the center
BOLD OR Strong. Strong is for emphasis/meaning, while bold is to label a keyword (ie- company name, person's name)

italics OR emphasis . Emphasis is to add emphasis, while italics is to mark an alternative text (ie- book title, person's thoughts, foreign language)

underlined OR insterted. Underline is ralely used, may be used to denote spelling error that should be fixed, etc. Inserted may be used for noting text that was ADDED. Also, rarely used. (Best to use 'text decoration' in CSS for underline (?)
